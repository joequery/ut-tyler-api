  • Typos in Homework #5 and #6

    Posted on: Monday, February 11, 2013

    Hi everyone,

    I was just made aware of a typo that I had made in Homework #5, and even copied over to Homework #6.  In problem #2 (on both homework #5 and #6), the group should be U(49) NOT U(40).  I realize this is a late catch for homework #5 and I will accept the answer that U(40) is not a cyclic group and thus has no generator.  However, I will not accept this for Homework #6 so be sure to change the group to U(49).   I strongly suggest to attempt problem #2 in Homework #5 with U(49) since I'll expect you to know how to do this type of thing for the test. It will be to your benefit to make the effort.  Sorry for catching this so late and thanks to the two students who brought this to my attention.  

    Dr. Stagg
